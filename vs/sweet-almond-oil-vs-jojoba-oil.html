The verdict

Sweet Almond Oil and Jojoba Oil score the same on the comedogenic scale (2/5), so for pore-clogging alone there is no clear winner. Pick on texture, price and how your own skin responds — and patch test.

Sweet Almond Oil: 2/5

Sweet Almond Oil rates 2/5 — a mild, low pore-clogging risk. Most skin tolerates it well; very reactive skin may want to patch test. It can also feed Malassezia, so people with fungal acne usually avoid it. Full breakdown: is Sweet Almond Oil comedogenic?
