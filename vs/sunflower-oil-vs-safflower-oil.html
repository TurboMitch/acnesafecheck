The verdict

Sunflower Oil and Safflower Oil score the same on the comedogenic scale (0/5), so for pore-clogging alone there is no clear winner. Pick on texture, price and how your own skin responds — and patch test.

Sunflower Oil: 0/5

Sunflower Oil rates 0/5 — non-comedogenic (acne safe). It is not known to clog pores and is generally a safe choice for acne-prone skin. It can also feed Malassezia, so people with fungal acne usually avoid it. Full breakdown: is Sunflower Oil comedogenic?
