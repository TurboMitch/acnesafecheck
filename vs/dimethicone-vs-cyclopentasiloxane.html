The verdict

Cyclopentasiloxane is the safer choice for acne-prone skin: it rates 0/5 (non-comedogenic (acne safe)) versus 1/5 for Dimethicone (a very low pore-clogging risk).

Dimethicone: 1/5

Dimethicone rates 1/5 — a very low pore-clogging risk. Most skin tolerates it well; very reactive skin may want to patch test. It is not a common fungal-acne (Malassezia) trigger. Full breakdown: is Dimethicone comedogenic?

Cyclopentasiloxane: 0/5

Cyclopentasiloxane rates 0/5 — non-comedogenic (acne safe). It is not known to clog pores and is generally a safe choice for acne-prone skin. It is not a common fungal-acne (Malassezia) trigger. Full breakdown: is Cyclopentasiloxane comedogenic?
