The verdict

Zinc Oxide and Titanium Dioxide score the same on the comedogenic scale (1/5), so for pore-clogging alone there is no clear winner. Pick on texture, price and how your own skin responds — and patch test.

Zinc Oxide: 1/5

Zinc Oxide rates 1/5 — a very low pore-clogging risk. Most skin tolerates it well; very reactive skin may want to patch test. It is not a common fungal-acne (Malassezia) trigger. Full breakdown: is Zinc Oxide comedogenic?
