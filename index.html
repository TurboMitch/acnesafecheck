Frequently asked questions

What does "comedogenic" mean?

Comedogenic ingredients tend to block pores, which can lead to comedones (blackheads and whiteheads) and breakouts. Ratings run from 0 (won't clog pores) to 5 (very likely to clog pores).

Is a high-rated ingredient always bad for me?

No. Many people use coconut oil or cocoa butter with no problems. Ratings are a starting point — concentration and your own skin matter a lot.

Where do the ratings come from?

From comedogenicity scales widely cited in the skincare community and dermatology references. We're transparent that these are estimates, not lab results for every formula.
