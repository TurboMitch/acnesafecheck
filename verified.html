Why brands get verified

"Non-comedogenic" is an unregulated marketing word, and acne-prone shoppers know it. An independent, ingredient-level check is far more convincing. Verified products get a third-party badge, a listing in our product directory, and link to a public report of the screened ingredient list.

How it works

  1. You send us the product's full INCI list.
  2. We screen every ingredient for pore-clogging (comedogenic 3+) and fungal-acne (Malassezia) triggers.
  3. If it passes, you receive the Acne-Safe Verified badge, a public report page, and a directory listing. If it doesn't, we tell you exactly which ingredients to reformulate.

What you get

  • The Acne-Safe Verified badge (web + print) for products that pass.
  • A public verification report page customers can click through to.
  • A listing in the AcneSafeCheck product directory and relevant roundups.
  • Re-screening when you reformulate.

Pricing

Verification is offered as a simple per-product or annual brand plan. Exact pricing depends on how many SKUs you want screened. Email us with your product range for a quote.

Note: Verification reflects an ingredient-level screen against published comedogenicity and Malassezia references; it is not a clinical or regulatory claim and not a guarantee for every individual's skin. See our methodology.
