What to look for

Acne-safe moisturizer formulas tend to be built around low-comedogenic ingredients such as Hyaluronic Acid, Glycerin, Niacinamide, Squalane, Dimethicone. Use our comedogenic ingredient checker to paste any product's list and confirm before you buy.

Ingredients to avoid

Be cautious with higher-rated pore-cloggers like Isopropyl Myristate, Coconut Oil, Cocoa Butter, Algae Extract. One of these near the top of the ingredient list is a red flag for breakout-prone skin.

FAQ

Does "non-comedogenic" actually mean anything?

It usually means rated 0 on the comedogenic scale, but it isn't a regulated term — a product can be labelled non-comedogenic and still contain a pore-clogger. That's why we check the real ingredient list. Learn more on what comedogenic means.
