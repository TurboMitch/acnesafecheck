What to look for

Acne-safe concealer formulas tend to be built around low-comedogenic ingredients such as Dimethicone, Salicylic Acid, Niacinamide. Use our comedogenic ingredient checker to paste any product's list and confirm before you buy.

Ingredients to avoid

Be cautious with higher-rated pore-cloggers like Isopropyl Myristate, Octyl Palmitate. One of these near the top of the ingredient list is a red flag for breakout-prone skin.

FAQ

Does "non-comedogenic" actually mean anything?

It usually means rated 0 on the comedogenic scale, but it isn't a regulated term — a product can be labelled non-comedogenic and still contain a pore-clogger. That's why we check the real ingredient list. Learn more on what comedogenic means.
