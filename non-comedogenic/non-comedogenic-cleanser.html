What to look for

Acne-safe cleanser formulas tend to be built around low-comedogenic ingredients such as Glycerin, Niacinamide, Salicylic Acid. Use our comedogenic ingredient checker to paste any product's list and confirm before you buy.

Ingredients to avoid

Be cautious with higher-rated pore-cloggers like Sodium Lauryl Sulfate, Isopropyl Myristate. One of these near the top of the ingredient list is a red flag for breakout-prone skin.

FAQ

Does "non-comedogenic" actually mean anything?

It usually means rated 0 on the comedogenic scale, but it isn't a regulated term — a product can be labelled non-comedogenic and still contain a pore-clogger. That's why we check the real ingredient list. Learn more on what comedogenic means.
