Is Zinc Oxide comedogenic?

Zinc Oxide is a UV filter used in sunscreens. Its comedogenicity rating is 1/5 — a very low pore-clogging risk.

1/5

Comedogenicity scale: 0 = won't clog pores, 5 = very likely to clog pores.
