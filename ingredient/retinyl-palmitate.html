Is Retinyl Palmitate comedogenic?

Quick answer: Retinyl Palmitate has a comedogenicity rating of 2/5 — a mild, low pore-clogging risk. It is also a common fungal-acne (Malassezia) trigger.

2/5
A mild, low pore-clogging risk
Comedogenicity scale: 0 = won't clog pores, 5 = very likely to clog pores.

Retinyl Palmitate carries only a mild pore-clogging risk. It is fine for most skin types; very reactive or acne-prone skin may still want to patch test a new product that contains it.

Does Retinyl Palmitate clog pores?

Rarely — Retinyl Palmitate has only a mild pore-clogging risk (rated 2/5) and suits most skin types, though very acne-prone skin may want to patch test. How to read this rating
