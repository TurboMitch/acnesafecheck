Is Tamanu Oil comedogenic?

Tamanu Oil is a plant or carrier oil used to moisturise and condition skin. Its comedogenicity rating is 2/5 — a mild, low pore-clogging risk.

2/5
A mild, low pore-clogging risk
Comedogenicity scale: 0 = won't clog pores, 5 = very likely to clog pores.

Tamanu Oil carries only a mild pore-clogging risk. It is fine for most skin types; very reactive or acne-prone skin may still want to patch test a new product that contains it.

What is Tamanu Oil?

Tamanu Oil is a plant or carrier oil used to moisturise and condition skin. On a product label it may also appear under names such as: calophyllum inophyllum seed oil, tamanu oil.

How to read this rating
