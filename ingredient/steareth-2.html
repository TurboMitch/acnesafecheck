Is Steareth-2 comedogenic?

Steareth-2 is an emulsifier that keeps oil and water mixed in a formula. Its comedogenicity rating is 2/5 — a mild, low pore-clogging risk.

2/5
A mild, low pore-clogging risk
Comedogenicity scale: 0 = won't clog pores, 5 = very likely to clog pores.

Steareth-2 carries only a mild pore-clogging risk. It is fine for most skin types; very reactive or acne-prone skin may still want to patch test a new product that contains it.

How to read this rating
