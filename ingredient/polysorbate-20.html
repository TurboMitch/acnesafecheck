Is Polysorbate 20 comedogenic?

Polysorbate 20 is an emulsifier that keeps oil and water mixed in a formula. Its comedogenicity rating is 0/5 — non-comedogenic (acne safe).

0/5

Comedogenicity scale: 0 = won't clog pores, 5 = very likely to clog pores.

Polysorbate 20 is considered non-comedogenic — it is not known to clog pores and is generally a safe choice for acne-prone skin.
