Is Laureth-23 comedogenic?

Laureth-23 is an emulsifier that keeps oil and water mixed in a formula. Its comedogenicity rating is 3/5 — moderate pore-clogging risk.

3/5
Moderate pore-clogging risk
Comedogenicity scale: 0 = won't clog pores, 5 = very likely to clog pores.

Laureth-23 has a moderate chance of clogging pores. Plenty of people tolerate it, but if you break out easily it is worth watching — particularly if it is one of the first few ingredients listed.

How to read this rating
