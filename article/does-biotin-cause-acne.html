Does biotin cause acne?

Short answer: Possibly at high supplement doses.

Biotin (vitamin B7) is widely taken for hair and nails, and high-dose biotin supplements are anecdotally linked to breakouts. The proposed mechanism is that large amounts of biotin may compete with pantothenic acid (B5) for absorption, and B5 is involved in skin health. The evidence is limited and mostly anecdotal.

Biotin from food is not a concern; this is about mega-dose supplements (often 5,000–10,000 mcg).

What you can do

  • If you started a biotin supplement and then broke out, trial stopping it.
  • You rarely need high-dose biotin unless a doctor advised it.
  • Reassess all new supplements when troubleshooting acne.
